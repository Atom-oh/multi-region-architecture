# VELLURE Design System: Prime Horizon

## 1. Brand Identity & Vision
**VELLURE** is a premium, architectural e-commerce brand that blends the high-efficiency information density of Amazon with a modern, curated aesthetic. The "Prime Horizon" system focuses on structural clarity, typographic precision, and a sophisticated color palette that elevates everyday shopping into a gallery-like experience.

## 2. Visual Principles
- **Architectural Clarity:** Use of clear lines, structured grids, and a persistent left-hand sidebar navigation to anchor the user experience.
- **Information Efficiency:** High density of information (like the "Buy Box" and detailed product specs) presented with clean whitespace to maintain readability.
- **Curated Transitions:** Smooth hover states and subtle tonal shifts that guide the eye without distracting from the products.

## 3. Color Palette
The palette is rooted in deep, authoritative tones complemented by high-contrast accents for action.

- **Primary Navy (#232f3e):** Used for global navigation, headers, and primary branding to convey stability and trust.
- **Accent Orange (#d88100):** A modern refinement of the signature commerce orange, reserved exclusively for primary CTAs like "Add to Cart" and "Buy Now."
- **Neutral Grey Scale:**
    - Surface: `#ffffff` (Pure White)
    - Sidebar/Secondary Background: `#f1f4f4`
    - Borders/Dividers: `#e6e9e9`
    - Text (Body): `#291400` (Deep Charred Wood)
    - Text (Secondary): `#535f70`

## 4. Typography
- **Primary Font: Plus Jakarta Sans**
    - Chosen for its modern, geometric clarity.
    - **Headlines:** Bold/Black weights with tight tracking (-0.02em) for an "architectural" feel.
    - **Product Titles:** Medium/Semi-bold, 24px-32px range.
- **Secondary Font: Inter**
    - Used for long-form body text, product descriptions, and technical specifications for maximum legibility at small sizes.

## 5. UI Components
### 5.1 Navigation
- **Top Bar:** Fixed, high-density search and utility bar in Primary Navy.
- **Left Sidebar:** Persistent navigation containing categories, user account links, and "Elite" status prompts. Uses a `#f1f4f4` background for clear visual hierarchy.

### 5.2 The "Buy Box"
- A floating or docked container on the right side of product pages.
- Features a clear price hierarchy, delivery estimates, and high-contrast Accent Orange buttons.
- Rounded corners set to `ROUND_FOUR` (4px-8px) for a professional, slightly sharp edge.

### 5.3 Product Cards
- Clean image presentation with minimal borders.
- Hover states include a subtle lift or shadow increase.

## 6. Layout Strategy
- **Desktop:** Two-column layout (Sidebar + Main Content). Main content utilizes a flexible grid for product listings.
- **Spacing:** Based on an 8px grid system to ensure consistent alignment and rhythm across all pages.
